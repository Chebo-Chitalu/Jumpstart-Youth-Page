About Us

VISION STATEMENT

To see a transformed; Vibrant, Spirit-filled, Word-built youth dispensing their God-given gifts, talents and callings. Initiating Impact and productivity in their communities and fostering an atmosphere of love, unity, and peace as Christ called us all to live.

MISSION STATEMENT

Drive change through community service, evangelism, projects, career development and business support, and sports. Creating inclusive programs for youth from all walks of life. Partnering and networking with various fellowships in our community.
